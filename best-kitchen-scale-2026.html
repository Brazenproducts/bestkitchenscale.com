How We Tested These Scales

We tested each scale across six scenarios:

  1. Calibration check: Compared against a certified 100g calibration weight and known-weight objects at 500g and 1,000g.
  2. Tare function accuracy: Tared with a bowl, added known weights incrementally, verified each reading.
  3. Low-weight precision: Weighed small amounts (3g, 5g, 10g) to test behavior at the low end.
  4. Repeatability: Removed and replaced the same 250g weight 10 times, checking for consistency.
  5. Display readability: Tested in bright kitchen light, dim conditions, and with large bowls covering parts of the display.
  6. Real baking use: Baked bread, cookies, and weighed proteins over several weeks of actual cooking.
