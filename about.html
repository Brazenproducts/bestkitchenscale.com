About BestKitchenScale.com

Independent kitchen scale reviews, honest rankings, and full transparency about how we make money.

Who We Are

BestKitchenScale.com is an independent product review site focused on one category: kitchen and food scales. We're a small team of home cooks, bakers, and nutrition-tracking enthusiasts who got tired of generic "best kitchen scale" lists that clearly hadn't used any of the products they were recommending.

We started this site to give honest, practical reviews based on actual use — not recycled specs from Amazon product pages. We bake bread, weigh food for macro tracking, brew coffee with scale-measured ratios, and meal prep weekly. Scales are part of our daily cooking lives.

How We Review

Our review process includes:

  • Calibration testing against known weights to verify accuracy claims
  • Real-world cooking and baking over multiple weeks with each scale
  • Tare function testing with multiple incremental weight additions
  • Display readability in various lighting conditions and with different bowl sizes
  • Long-term use assessment for build quality and durability
  • User review analysis — we read hundreds of verified buyer reviews to surface common failure modes and long-term ownership experiences

We don't accept free products from manufacturers for review. Every scale on our list was purchased independently or is a product we already owned and used.

FTC Affiliate Disclosure

As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. When you click a product link on BestKitchenScale.com and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we fund the site, cover testing costs, and keep the content updated.

We are required by the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) to disclose this relationship. We take this disclosure seriously and include it on every page of the site, not just buried in a footer.

Our affiliate relationships do not influence our rankings. We rank scales based on real-world performance, accuracy, value, and fit for the use cases we cover. We would recommend the same scales whether or not they offered us a commission.

We do not accept:

  • Paid placements or sponsored rankings
  • Payment to include or exclude specific products
  • Free products in exchange for positive reviews
  • "Sponsored content" that isn't clearly labeled as advertising

Our Standards

  • We only recommend products we'd use ourselves or gift to a family member
  • We update our content when products are discontinued, when better options emerge, or when our testing reveals new information
  • We disclose all material connections to products and brands
  • We correct errors promptly when we make them
